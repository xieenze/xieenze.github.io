Enze Xie (谢恩泽)

I am a PhD student in The University of Hong Kong (HKU) since 2019.10, supervised by Dr. Ping Luo. I am also a research intern at Sensetime. Before that, I was a research intern at Face++ Detection Group, supervised by Mr. Shuai Shao and Dr. Gang Yu. I also work very close with my friend Wenhai Wang and Prof. Chunhua Shen. I obtained my bachelor degree from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (南京航空航天大学) and master degree from TongJi University (同济大学).

My research interest includes computer vision and deep learning.
